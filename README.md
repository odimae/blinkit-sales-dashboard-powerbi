# Blinkit Sales Dashboard — Power BI

An interactive sales analytics dashboard built in Power BI 
analyzing Blinkit grocery outlet performance across outlet 
types, locations, item categories and fat content.

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Total Sales | $1.2M |
| Average Rating | 3.92 |
| Total Items | 8,523 |
| Total Outlets | 4 |

---

## Visuals Included

- **Sales by Year** — line chart showing sales trend over time
- **Total Sales by Outlet Type** — bar chart comparing Supermarket Type1, Grocery Store, Type2, Type3
- **Sales by Outlet Location** — donut chart by Tier 1, Tier 2, Tier 3
- **Sales by Item Type** — funnel chart across 16 item categories
- **Sales by Outlet Size** — donut chart by High, Medium, Small
- **Sales by Item Fat Content** — donut chart Low Fat vs Regular
- **Summary Table** — outlet type breakdown by sales, items, visibility and weight

---

## Filters / Slicers

- Year
- Outlet Type
- Outlet Location Type (Tier 1 / 2 / 3)
- Item Fat Content

---

## Insights from the Data

- Supermarket Type1 dominates with $787,550 in sales — 65% of total revenue
- Regular fat content products outsell Low Fat at 64.6% vs 35.4%
- Tier 2 locations lead sales at 39.27% followed closely by Tier 3 and Tier 1
- Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods are the top selling item categories
- High outlet size generates the most revenue at $507.9K (42.27%)

---

## Tools Used

- Power BI Desktop
- DAX for calculated measures
- Power Query for data transformation

---

## Dataset

The dataset contains grocery outlet sales data including:
- Item details (type, fat content, weight, visibility)
- Outlet details (type, size, location tier, establishment year)
- Sales figures per item per outlet

---

## How to Open

1. Download the `.pbix` file
2. Open in **Power BI Desktop** (free download from Microsoft)
3. All visuals and filters are interactive
